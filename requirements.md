# HelpNest Requirements

## Overview
HelpNest is an AI-powered civic support platform designed to improve access to government schemes, legal rights, and emergency services. It uses AI to interpret user problems, simplify complex information, and guide users to verified official resources.

## Functional Requirements
- AI assistant capable of understanding natural language queries.
- Personalized recommendations for government schemes, legal guidance, and emergency services.
- Simplification of legal and administrative language for easier understanding.
- Real-time 24/7 support.
- Secure and private handling of user data.

## Non-functional Requirements
- Fast and responsive system with low latency for real-time assistance.
- Scalable architecture to handle multiple simultaneous users.
- Compliance with data privacy regulations.
- Intuitive and user-friendly interface for both web and mobile platforms.
- Responsible AI practices to ensure fairness, transparency, and accuracy.
